8 Week SQL Challenge

The 8 Week SQL Challenge is a series of 8 challenging case studies where SQL is needed to evaluate different scenarios such as how much did each customer spend at a restaurant, what is the total revenue of products purchased, and how many customers does a bank have. It requires advanced knowledge of SQL to execute Common Table Expressions (CTEs), joins, and window functions.

More information can be found at 8 Week SQL Challenge.
